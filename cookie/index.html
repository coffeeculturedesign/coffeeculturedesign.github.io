Cookies Policy

Last Update: 12 September 2024

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time you visit. Cookies can be set by the website you are visiting (first-party cookies) or by third-party services like analytics and advertising networks (third-party cookies). Their primary function is to enhance your browsing experience and help websites perform certain actions efficiently.

How we use cookies

We use cookies primarily to store an approximate location of the user to automatically set the appropriate region for browsing, ensuring that content and prices are relevant to the user’s location. These cookies improve user experience by streamlining region-specific settings, so you don’t have to manually select your region every time you visit the site. Additionally, cookies help us analyze user behavior on our website, allowing us to improve site performance and usability. Some cookies also serve marketing purposes by enabling personalized ads. The cookies we use generally last for 30 days, after which they expire.

Your choices about cookies

You have the ability to control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, and you can also set preferences for receiving notifications when cookies are being used. Keep in mind, however, that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our site, such as needing to manually choose your region or losing saved preferences. You can also opt out of cookies used for personalized advertising or analytics by visiting third-party opt-out platforms.

How long do cookies last

The duration of cookies varies depending on their type. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you choose to delete them. Our cookies, which store the approximate location for regional settings, last for 30 days. Third-party cookies, such as those used for analytics or advertising, may have different expiration periods, as determined by their respective providers.
